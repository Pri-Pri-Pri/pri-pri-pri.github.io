Priscilla Posada

JANE OF ALL TRADES

As a Macaulay Honors Scholar at the City University of New York, I researched emergent technologies in Cuba (back in 2009!) and their impact on civil society. From there, I worked at Google and several startups, both in the U.S. and Germany, in roles where the TL;DR version would read: “explaining technical concepts to non-technical audiences.” Working side-by-side engineers exposed me to coding as a creative practice and I’ve been designing online experiences ever since. In a parallel life, I’m a literary translator—I love language both human and machine.
