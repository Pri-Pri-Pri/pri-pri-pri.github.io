Priscilla Posada

PUBLICIST

One afternoon in a library in Berlin, I discovered a novel by the experimental Argentine writer, Pablo Katchadjian. Right then and there, I knew that I had to translate this book into English so more people could read it. I pitched it to Dalkey Archive Press and now the book exists as What to Do.
There’s really nothing I love more than rallying behind the people and projects I believe in. As a publicist, I get to do just that. My current work ranges from promoting brilliant designers to finding an audience for filmmakers I admire. And, if it takes translating something myself, I’ll do just that.
